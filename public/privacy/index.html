Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 January 2025  ·  Last updated: 18 March 2025

4th TOTS International Pelvic Course — Pre-course E-Learning Platform

Summary: We collect only what is necessary to provide your e-learning experience. Your data is used to track course progress, issue completion certificates, and improve the platform. We do not sell your data to third parties.

01

Information We Collect

When you sign in to the platform using your Google account, we collect:

  • Account information: your name, email address, and profile picture as provided by Google.
  • Course progress data: which lessons you have completed and when.
  • Video resume positions: the playback position in each video so you can resume where you left off.
  • Access logs: the date and time you accessed the platform.

We do not collect payment information, physical addresses, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

02

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity and grant access to course materials.
  • Save and restore your lesson progress across devices and sessions.
  • Send a Certificate of Completion to your email address when you finish all lessons.
  • Display your name and profile picture in the course interface.
  • Notify course administrators of completion rates (aggregate, anonymised where possible).
03

Data Storage

Your progress and resume data are stored in Google Firestore, a cloud database operated by Google LLC. All data is stored in Google's secure infrastructure and is protected by Google's enterprise-grade security measures.

Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) and Google Sign-In. We do not store your Google password.

A one-time record of certificate delivery is stored locally in your browser's localStorage to prevent duplicate emails.

04

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Your data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Google LLC: as our infrastructure provider for authentication and database services.
  • Course administrators: to verify completion for CME/CPD credit purposes if applicable.
  • Legal requirements: if required by applicable law or court order.
05

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data by updating your Google account profile.
  • Delete your course data by using the "Reset Course" feature within the platform.
  • Withdraw consent by signing out and ceasing to use the platform.

To request full deletion of your data from our systems, please contact us using the details below.

06

Cookies & Local Storage

This platform uses browser sessionStorage to maintain your authentication session during your visit (cleared automatically when you close your browser tab) and localStorage to record whether your certificate has been sent.

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

07

Children's Privacy

This platform is intended for medical professionals and is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact the course administration team.
